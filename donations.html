Step-by-Step Instructions for Donating to Code For Good

  1. Visit the Donation Page
    • Donate to Code For Good
  2. Fill Out Contact Information
    • Enter your email address and phone number in the field labeled "Contact Information."
  3. Enter the Student Organization Name
    • In the field labeled "Student Organization Name," type Code For Good to ensure your donation is directed to our organization!
  4. Specify the Reason for Payment
    • From the dropdown menu labeled "Reason for Payment," select the appropriate reason for your donation.
  5. Provide the Name of the Donor
    • Enter the name of the person or entity making the payment in the designated field.
  6. Choose Your Payment Method
    • Select your preferred payment option:
      • Card: Provide your card number, expiration date, CVC, and cardholder name.
      • Google Pay: Select this option if you want to pay using your Google Pay account.
      • Bank: Choose this if you prefer paying directly from your bank account.
  7. Enter Billing Address (if required)
    • Depending on your payment method, you may need to provide your billing address. Follow the prompts and complete the fields as necessary.
  8. Optional: Save Your Payment Info
    • If you'd like, you can check the box to save your information for faster checkout in the future through Link.
  9. Review Your Donation Details
    • Double-check all the information you entered, including the donation amount, student organization name, and payment details.
  10. Submit Your Donation
    • Click the Pay button at the bottom of the page to complete your donation. You will see a confirmation message if your payment is successful.
  11. Confirmation Email and Virtual Donor Wall Invitation
    • After your donation, you will receive a confirmation email from ASUC.
    • You will also receive an email from Code For Good asking if you would like to be featured on our Virtual Donor Wall.
